Info

Rome is the capital of Italy. It is located on the Tiber River. The population of the city is about 3 million people.

Rome is an ancient city. It was founded more than 2.5 thousand years ago. It was the capital of the Roman Empire in the past.

There are many attractions in Rome. Tourists from all over the world come to see the Colosseum, the Pantheon, the Roman Forum and other architectural monuments.

The Vatican is located in Rome. Interestingly, the Vatican is a separate small state.

Tourists are attracted not only by the architecture of the city, but also by Italian cuisine. There are many cafes, restaurants and pizzerias in Rome. There is even a Pasta Museum.
